AI Integration

Your circuit,
analyzed by AI

The Sparky AI panel reads your live breadboard state — components, wires, connections — and feeds it directly to IBM watsonx Granite. In seconds you get actionable, board-specific feedback.

  • Identifies wiring errors and polarity mistakes before you burn an LED
  • Suggests the single most important next step to complete your circuit
  • Explains what each component does in plain, beginner-friendly language
  • Works in real time — the board JSON is captured fresh on every request
Sparky AI — watsonx
What's wrong with my circuit?
Issue found: Your LED (led_0) has no current-limiting resistor between pin e14 and the power rail. At 9V this will burn it out. Add a 330Ω resistor between the positive rail and hole e14 before powering up.
What should I do next?
Place a resistor from hole a14 → a18, then wire tp_14 → a14. That gives you a proper current-limited LED circuit. You're one wire away from a working circuit! ⚡
