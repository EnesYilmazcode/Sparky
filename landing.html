3D Breadboard Visualization

AI Circuit Tutor

Ask the built-in AI tutor anything. It reads your board state and can build or fix circuits on your behalf.

Live Simulation

Run your circuit instantly. LEDs light up, currents are calculated, and short circuits are caught automatically.

Click any two holes to draw a wire. Wires snap to breadboard holes and rails with precise color coding.

Export your circuit as a .sparky file and reload it anytime. Share designs with anyone.
